CrowdFunding

Crowdfunding is the practice of funding a project
or venture by raising many small amounts of money
from a large number of people, typically via the Internet.

Kickstarter

The funding goal is the amount of money that a creator needs to complete their project. Funding on Kickstarter is all-or-nothing. No one will be charged for a pledge towards a project unless it reaches its funding goal.

GoFundMe.

GoFundMe is unique in that it allows anyone to create a request for anything. While both KickStarter and IndieGoGo require a tangible product delivery, funds raised by a GoFundMe campaign can be used for services such as paying hospital bills or student debt.
